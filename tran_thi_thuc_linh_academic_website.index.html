About

Academic profile, education and professional experience.

Biography

Dr. Tran Thi Thuc Linh received the B.Eng. degree in Electronics and Telecommunications from Hanoi University of Science and Technology in 1999, the M.Sc. degree in Digital Information from Christian-Albrechts University at Kiel in 2005, and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering and Electrical Engineering from Curtin University in 2018.

She has been a lecturer at the Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology since 2000. From 2019 to 2020, she worked as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, where she conducted research in speech signal processing.
